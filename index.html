What are Third Culture Kids?

Third Culture Kids (TCKs) are young people who grow up between different cultures because of moving between countries. Many TCKs struggle with belonging and identity, but they also develop unique global experiences and perspectives.

Experiences🎢

Amira — From Kenya & Canada

“I moved between Nairobi and Toronto during my childhood. Sometimes I feel like I belong to both places, but also neither at the same time.”

Lucas — From Brazil & Japan

“Changing schools often was difficult for me. I learned many cultures, but saying goodbye repeatedly was emotionally exhausting.”

Sofia — From France & Ghana

“People always ask me where I am really from. It made me question my identity for a long time.”
